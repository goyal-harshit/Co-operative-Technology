Empowering Rural Communities

Overview

The Bhiwani District Primary Co-op. Agri. & Rural Dev. Bank Ltd. (PCARDB) meets the financial needs of farmers and rural people. This bank supports agriculture and rural development by providing loans and other financial services. We were established in 1920 and has been serving the people of Bhiwani since then. The bank offers loans for crops, buying equipment, and other needs. Additionally, it supports small businesses in rural areas.

Our Mission and Goals

Helping Farmers Grow

Giving money help for growing crops, setting up irrigation, and using machines.

Supporting Rural Development

Helping with rural roads, small industries, and local businesses.

Financial Inclusion

Making sure everyone in the village can get loans and other money services.

Sustainable Development

Encouraging good farming practices and taking care of the environment.

Empowering Members

Teaching and training members to improve their skills and abilities.

Our Services

Agricultural Loans

Short-term, medium-term, and long-term loans for various agricultural purposes.

Rural Development Loans

Financing for rural infrastructure projects like roads, schools, and healthcare facilities.

Microfinance

Small loans for rural entrepreneurs to start or expand micro-enterprises.

Savings Accounts

Encouraging rural savings through various deposit schemes.

Insurance Services

Crop insurance and other risk management products to protect farmers.

Technical Assistance

Providing guidance and training on modern agricultural practices and technologies.

Technologies We Use

Agri-Tech Solutions

Helping farmers with weather updates, market prices, and advice.
